Thank you for using Note App. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

1. Data We Collect

Note App does NOT collect any personal data.

All notes and data are saved locally on your device using internal storage or a local database. We do not transfer your data to any server or third party.

3. Third-Party Services

This app does not use any third-party services that may collect user data.

4. Children’s Privacy

Note App does not collect data from children under the age of 13.

5. Data Deletion

Since your notes are stored on your device, you can delete all your data by:
